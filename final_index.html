Kukkiwon Taekwondo

Kukkiwon Taekwondo is a martial arts studio dedicated to delivering fun and challenging Taekwondo activities to both kids and adults.

After School Program

The after-school program runs on weekdays 3:30 pm to 5:30 pm. It is an exciting way to keep kids busy and entertained while gaining valuable life skills and exercise. It will teach kids about discipline, respect, perseverance, and invaluable self-defense skills. Typical Taekwondo routines involve stretching, kicking and punching exercises, and practicing proper form and demonstrations. In addition to traditional Taekwondo routines, our program will give the kids a chance to make long lasting friendship with their peers through extracurricular activities like soccer, parkour, dodgeball and team building exercises.

Adult Program

The adult program runs twice a week on Saturdays and Sundays from 10:30 am to 12:30 pm and welcoming to all skill levels, genders, and age groups above 19. This program provides an excellent way to exercise both your body and mind. Participants will learn to develop a healthier lifestyle, increase flexibility, create more energy in your day and experience weight loss. Most adults in our program have a demanding lifestyle with work, study, and family. They use this program to relieve stress while gaining a friendly and supportive workout with their peers.
